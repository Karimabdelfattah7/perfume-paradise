Return & Exchange Policy

We want you to love your fragrance. If you're not completely satisfied, we accept returns or exchanges within 15 days of purchase with your original receipt. Items must be in their original condition.

  • 15-day return or exchange window from date of purchase
  • Original receipt required for all returns and exchanges
  • Items must be in original, unused condition
  • Bring item back in-store to Mall St. Mathews location

Authenticity Guarantee

Every fragrance we carry is 100% authentic, sourced directly from authorized distributors and brand partners. We stand behind the quality of every product we sell.

Payment & Security

We accept all major credit and debit cards, processed securely through Clover point-of-sale. Your payment information is encrypted and never stored on our servers.
